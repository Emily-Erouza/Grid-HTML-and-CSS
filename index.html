ABOUT CYBERSECURITY

Cybersecurity is about protecting computers, networks, and data from bad people who want to steal or damage them. I t includes measures to prevent hacking, viruses, and other threats. As we use technology more in our daily lives, it's important to keep our personal and business information safe. By using strong passwords, updating software, and being careful about sharing information online, we can help protect ourselves from cyber attacks.

To Stop Cyber Attacks

Cyber attacks are becoming more common and dangerous, affecting people, businesses, and governments.Experts are warning that we need to take strong steps to protect ourselves from threats like phishing, ransomware, and data breaches.

why it is important

Cybersecurity is important because it protects our personal information, financial data, and sensitive business details from being stolen or damaged by cybercriminals.

As we use the internet for banking, shopping, and communicating, strong cybersecurity measures help keep our data safe and ensure our online activities remain private and secure. Without good cybersecurity, we risk losing money, privacy, and trust in digital services.
